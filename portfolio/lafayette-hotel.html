Lafayette Hotel / Website Visit Site

Project: lafayettehotel.com website redesign
Date: Early 2013
What I did: web design, project management

The Lafayette Hotel is a charming riverboat era hotel in Marietta, Ohio. They needed a website that conveyed the old-time elegance of the hotel without seeming too stuffy or luxe—one that paid homage to the hotel's unique history but also encouraged visitors to book.

Recently I hired Jessica to design and develop a property related website for me. Throughout the project she provided clear and insightful recommendations, delivered superb project management, and was an absolute pleasure to work with. The results were amazing with online reservations shooting through the roof and overall really good feedback on the site from guests. I would definitely use her services again!

- Teresa L., Lafayette Hotel
