Mr. Bing / Website Visit Site

Project: Mr. Bing website design
Date: 2013
What I did: web design, code, project management

Mr. Bing is a restaurant serving Beijing-style jianbings in the heart of Hong Kong. This was really fun to design, because the branding is colorful and playful. I chose Nunito for the typeface, and decided to make the buttons extra touchable.

This was my first responsive website, and I built it without the help of any frameworks. (If I could do it over, I would definitely use a framework, and I would have designed it without the carousel in the jumbotron.)
